Olist customer behavior analysis.

From 100,000+ raw Brazilian e-commerce orders to a decision-ready Power BI dashboard.

SQL Power BI Excel

Creating a clear picture of Olist's revenue

The goal was to move beyond a "big table of transactions" and build a clear picture of which customers, categories, and sellers actually drive revenue and satisfaction.

The business questions

  • Which product categories generate the most revenue?
  • How do delivery delays impact review scores?
  • Which sellers contribute the largest share of overall revenue?
Role & Responsibilities

End-to-end project execution: data cleaning, SQL modeling, exploratory analysis, and Power BI dashboard design.
