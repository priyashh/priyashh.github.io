OLIST E-COMMERCE ANALYSIS

OVERVIEW

Analyzed 100k+ e-commerce orders dataset to understand revenue trends, customer behaviour, delivery performance, and seller contribution.

BUSINESS QUESTIONS

  • Which categories generate most revenue?
  • How delivery delay impacts review score?

ANALYSIS PERFORMED

  • SQL joins across 8 tables
  • Revenue trend analysis
  • Delivery performance analysis
  • Customer distribution by state
  • Payment behaviour insights
  • Seller performance comparison

KEY INSIGHTS

  • Few sellers contribute major share of revenue
  • Delivery delays reduce review scores
  • Customers prefer credit card installments
  • Revenue concentrated in few categories
