Story of
my life.

I am Priyash Vasava, an Analyst II based in Vadodara, Gujarat. With over two and a half years of experience, my focus is on removing the clutter of raw information to expose the core insights that drive industries forward.

My technical foundation was built at ITI Ankleshwar and expanded through a formative internship at the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO). I am currently further broadening my analytical perspective by pursuing a BA in Economics through Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Open University. This combination of strict engineering principles and economic theory allows me to build dashboards that are not just technically sound, but strategically valuable.

When I am not mastering SQL or designing Power BI interactions at Etech Global Services, my analytical mindset spills over into my personal pursuits. I am deeply engaged in 3D modeling and animation using Blender, currently developing a YouTube channel focused on data-driven storytelling and oddly satisfying visual loops. Off-screen, I am a huge fan of complex mystery and thriller narratives, and I have a growing interest in botany and local plant nurseries.
