Story of my life.

Hey! Hows it hangin?

This is Priyash Vasava, an Analyst II based in Vadodara, Gujarat. With over two and a half years of experience (and a formative year-long internship), my focus is on removing the clutter of raw information to expose the core insights that drive industries forward.

My technical foundation expanded through an internship at the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO), where I was introduced to advanced uses of Excel and reporting. I am also currently completing my degree in Economics. I am a vibe coder (directing AI to bridge the gap between intent and code) and my curiosity has no limits (or that's what I like to think).

From a young age wondering how the earth spins, to understanding how anything functions at all, I was always drawn to computers and the science behind them. Like many of us, things didn't perfectly align for me early in my career, and I had very few mentors to guide me—until the era of Artificial Intelligence took place.

Now, there are many folks who learned all the technical stuff through traditional academia. But if I speak for myself, I feel no shame in saying that I learned SQL mostly using AI models, YouTube tutorials, and Reddit, knowing that ultimately it's all about how much I practice writing the code.

After SQL, I introduced myself to Power BI and Tableau. It turns out both of these tools are intuitive and require immense practice to master—and I did exactly that. I worked on countless unfinished personal projects that nobody will ever hear about, but for me, it was the learning that I took away.

I am a forever learner. By building the projects highlighted here, I am just trying to step into the arena, find the problems I can solve using what I have learned so far, and keep on growing.

Ciao.
